About Me

My name is Ben Caler. After spending years poking away at different programming languages I have finally enrolled in a full stack web development bootcamp. This class will teach me front-end and back-end web development and make me a highly qualified candidate for high demand jobs.

In my free time I enjoy spending time in the outdoors fishing, hunting, backpacking, or playing with my two kids. My son's name is Mason (4), and my daughter is Lily (2). They are the best kids a dad could ask for.
